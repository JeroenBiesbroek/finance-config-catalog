# Fixed Asset Rules

## Capitalization Threshold
Assets with an acquisition cost above EUR 500 (excl. VAT) are capitalized.
Assets below this threshold are expensed in the period of acquisition.

## Depreciation Methods

| Asset category | Method | Useful life | Residual value |
|---|---|---|---|
| IT equipment | Straight-line | 3 years | 0% |
| Office furniture | Straight-line | 5 years | 0% |
| Vehicles | Straight-line | 5 years | 10% |
| Software licenses | Straight-line | 3 years | 0% |
| Leasehold improvements | Straight-line | Lease term | 0% |

## Processing Rules

1. All additions require Finance Lead approval (CTRL-FA-003)
2. All disposals require Finance Lead approval (CTRL-FA-003)
3. Depreciation runs monthly as part of controlled close
4. Asset register is reviewed quarterly (CTRL-FA-001)
5. Depreciation output is reviewed monthly (CTRL-FA-002)

## Changes
Changes to these rules require Finance Lead + CFO approval.
