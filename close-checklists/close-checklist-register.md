# Close Checklist Register

## Purpose
Tracks completed close checklists by period for audit and reference.

## Register

| Period | Close started | Close completed | Finance Lead sign-off | CFO sign-off | Checklist location | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| _2026-03_ | _YYYY-MM-DD_ | _YYYY-MM-DD_ | _Name, Date_ | _Name, Date_ | _Link to archived checklist_ | _First close in new system_ |

> **Note:** Add a row for each completed period close. Archive the filled checklist and link it here.

## Archive Policy
- Completed checklists are stored for a minimum of 7 years
- Checklists may not be modified after final close sign-off
